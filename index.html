About Us

Rawal Health Centre was dedicated to providing accessible and reliable diagnostic services to the Rawaltown community. Our focus was on accurate testing, patient comfort, and fast results.

Our Facilities

  • Digital X-Ray – Fast, low-radiation imaging for chest, bones, and joints
  • Pathology Lab – Fully equipped for blood and urine analysis
  • Cardiac Unit – ECG and basic cardiac screening
  • Ultrasound Room – Abdomen, pelvis, and pregnancy scans
  • Consultation Rooms – Private spaces for general checkups
